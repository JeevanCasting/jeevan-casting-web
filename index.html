About Us

Founded by Mumbai-based Casting Director Abhishek Kumar (Jeevan), Jeevan Casting is a dynamic agency dedicated to the art of discovery. We specialize in connecting visionary creators with the perfect faces and the finest actors in the industry.

With a powerful nationwide network and a team of seasoned professionals, we streamline the casting process for Hindi Feature Films, Web Series, and Television Commercials. Our deep passion for the craft of acting ensures that every project we touch is backed by talent that truly brings the story to life.

Television Commercials
